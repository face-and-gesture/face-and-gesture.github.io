Call for Sponsors

IEEE Conference on Automatic Face and Gesture Recognition is the most authoritative international professional conference in the field of image and video-based face recognition, gesture recognition, and human gesture recognition. The 17th IEEE International Conference on Automatic Face and Gesture Recognition (FG 2023) will be co-located with WACV 2023 at the Waikoloa Beach Marriott Resort, Hawaii, USA from June 4- June 8, 2023. FG 2023 International Conference covers many fields of automatic face and gesture recognition and its applications, including computer vision, pattern recognition, computer graphics, machine learning related to face recognition, gesture recognition, and human gesture recognition, new algorithms and applications, etc.

We sincerely invite you to support and sponsor FG 2023! This is an excellent opportunity to show and publicize your cooperation. In addition, through product displays and demonstrations, more experts, scholars, and students in the field of automatic face and gesture recognition can better understand your brand!

FG 2023 international conference includes four levels: Platinum sponsor, gold sponsor, silver sponsor, and bronze sponsor.

Your support and sponsorship are crucial to the success of FG 2023 International Conference! Depending on the amount of sponsorship, the Organizing Committee of the conference will ensure that sponsors will enjoy different levels of services provided by the organizer.

Platinum sponsor (over US $10000)

+Complimentary registration to the conference for 3 participants;

+Name Test of Time Award;

+Special thanks for the welcome dinner;

+The sponsor’s trademark will be placed on the conference website, manuals, and conference proceedings;

+ 1 complimentary exhibit stand for free

Gold medal sponsor (more than US $5000)

+ Complimentary registration to the conference for 2 participants;

+Name Best paper award;

+Special thanks for the welcome dinner;

+The sponsor’s trademark will be placed on the conference website, manuals, and conference proceedings;

+1 complimentary exhibit stand.

Silver sponsor (more than US $3000)

+Complimentary registration to the conference for 1 participant;

+Name Best Student Paper award;

+Special thanks for the welcome dinner;

+The sponsor’s trademark will be placed on the conference website, manuals, and conference proceedings.

Bronze sponsor (over US $1500)

+ Complimentary registration to the conference for 1 participant;

+The sponsor’s trademark is placed on the conference website, manuals, and Conference Papers Collection.

Award setting: This FG 2023 conference has set up three Best Paper Awards named by the platinum sponsor, gold medal sponsor, and silver sponsor respectively: Test of Time award, Best paper award, and Best Student paper award. These three awards will be praised at FG 2023 Conference. The title right is preferred to be given to the sponsor with the highest sponsorship amount of the same level.

Once sponsored, the organizing committee will place the sponsor’s trademark on the conference website http://hal.cse.msu.edu/fg2023/ immediately to help it attract more attention.

For sponsorship of the conference, please contact the sponsorship chairs of FG 2023 conference:

  • Stephanie Schuckers - Clarkson University, USA
